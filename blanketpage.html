Blanket Patterns

You Will Need.

    Yarn
    a bulky size 8 yarn is ideal
    crochet hook
    a size 6-10 mm hook works best
  • scissors
  • measuring tape

Green Blanket
chain 150, turn
Double crochet to end of row
Double Crochet to end of row, repeat until row 20
Back Loop Double Crochet only for another 20 rows
Regular Double Crochet for the next 20 rows
fasten off
