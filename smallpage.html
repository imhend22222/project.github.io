Small Patterns

You Will Need.

    Yarn
    a size 3-4 yarn is ideal
    crochet hook
    a size your yarn suggests works best
  • scissors
  • measuring tape

Pink Bookmark
chain 25, turn
sc, chain 2, sk1, sc, repeat to end
3 sc in each ch 2 space until end of row
chain 20 for tail
cut some yarn for end of tail.
